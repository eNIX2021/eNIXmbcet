ABOUT

The Electronics Association is an active with entity. Enthusiastic participation from the students of all semesters.

The association focuses on continuous learning, team building, and sequential knowledge upgradation.

The fuctioning of eNIX is predominantly handled by the student committee and all the students of ECE department, with the guidance of our HoD Dr. Dr. Jayakumari J and staff co-ordinator Ms. Anu Susan Philip.

VISION

To enable the ECE department to produce well rounded professionals and expand into a domain of personal and industrial relevance.

MISSION

The upskilling and overall technical and non-technical development of students of ECE department.

29th April 2020

Plastic Eradication Awareness Campaign

Spandanam team, in association with UBA (Unnath Bharat Abhiyan) cell visited St. John’s Medical Village, Pirappancode. The team split into groups of 4, with a faculty accompanying each. Each team took surveys on education, health sanitation, electricity, etc, and elucidated why plastic eradication is the need of the hour. Each household were gifted cloth bags that were creatively crafted by the students themselves.

Quaestio

To instil a sense of desire within the students to always expand their knowledge, the electronics association of MBCET, ‘eNIX’, conducted a quiz competition ‘QUAESTIO’ among the ECE students at Vishwesaraya hall on 16 th November 2019. Vishnu C Nair and Sreehari of S7 EC1 bagged the first position, closely followed by C. Harikrishna Sharma and Karthik Pradeep of S3 EC2 at second position and Gokul G.S and Ashwin Sharma of S3 EC2 securing the third position. Dr. M.J Jayashree, Head of the Department of ECE, awarded the prize money and the certificates to all the winners
